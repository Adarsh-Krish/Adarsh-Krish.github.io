Profile Summary

As a highly motivated and enthusiastic software engineer, I have always been passionate about pursuing my career in the field of computer science. My journey in this domain began when I completed my higher secondary education, specializing in Physics, Chemistry, and Maths, along with Computer Science. I then went on to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of Calicut, where I graduated with First Class and was the class topper. My academic excellence in the field of computer science has fueled my desire to continue learning and growing in this field.

I am currently pursuing an MSc in Advanced Computer Science at the University of Hertfordshire, with a focused mindset on building a strong academic and practical profile to ultimately become a successful software engineer in the future. My education has equipped me with the knowledge and skills required to succeed as a software engineer. I have a range of technical skills, including programming in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, Flutter, Java, Android, and MySQL. Additionally, I have a basic appreciation of Python, R language, C++, React JS, Node JS, MongoDB, JUnit Testing, and C programming.

Apart from my academic and professional pursuits, I enjoy various hobbies such as drawing, playing video games, swimming, playing the guitar, and crafting. These hobbies allow me to take a break from my work and engage in activities that I am passionate about. I believe that having a well-rounded life outside of work is essential for maintaining a healthy work-life balance.
